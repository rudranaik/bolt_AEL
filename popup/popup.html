About Activity & Emotion Logger

This extension helps you track what you're doing and how you're feeling throughout your day. Get regular check-ins to maintain awareness of your activities and emotions.

Key Features

  • Periodic activity and emotion logging
  • Customizable reminder frequency
  • Detailed activity history
  • Data export capabilities
  • Customizable emotion labels
